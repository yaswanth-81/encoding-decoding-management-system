College Exam Evaluation System

The Coding and Decoding Management System is designed specifically for college exam evaluations, ensuring secure and unbiased grading by replacing hall ticket numbers with encoded values.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to streamline the examination process by implementing an encoding and decoding system that maintains anonymity in student evaluations, reducing bias and ensuring fair assessments.

Features and Capabilities

  • Automated Encoding: Convert hall ticket numbers into unique encoded IDs for secure evaluations.
  • Faculty Assignment: Assign faculty to exams without revealing student identities.
  • Seamless Decoding: Decode the encoded numbers after evaluation to assign grades correctly.

Why Choose Our System

  • Security: Ensures anonymity and prevents biased grading.
  • Efficiency: Automates the encoding and decoding process for a streamlined exam workflow.
  • Reliability: Designed for accurate and error-free exam evaluations.
